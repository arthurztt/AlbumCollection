Freewave 3 by LUCKI

This is the third tape and first album in LUCKI’s Freewave tape collection. With Freewave 3, LUCKI goes in-depth on his personal struggles and successes with drugs, women, and fake love.

The album is tied to a year of LUCKI’s life while he had moved from Chicago to NYC to be with a woman.
